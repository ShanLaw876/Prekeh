Why SoleSync?

Buying shoes shouldn't mean paying for a second shoe you'll never use. Whether you use a prosthetic, have a limb difference, or only need one shoe, today's retail experience wasn't designed with you in mind.

How it works

  1. Browse and find the shoe you want
  2. We match you with someone who needs the opposite foot of the shoe
  3. Receive a brand-new shoe without buying an unnecessary pair
